Day 12 — Inclined Planes

An inclined plane changes how gravity accelerates an object. Adjust the angle of the ramp and see how the component of gravity along the slope changes the object's acceleration. Connect this motion to calculus by observing how acceleration determines the rate at which velocity changes.
